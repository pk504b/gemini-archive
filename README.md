# Gemini Archive

Archive and declutter your Google Gemini conversations without deleting them permanently.

Google Gemini is a powerful tool we all love and use everyday. Its sidebar, however, can quickly become cluttered. Currently, your only choice is to keep a chat visible or delete it permanently. Gemini Archive fills this gap by giving you a non-destructive way to hide chats you don’t need right now, without losing the valuable information within them.

## Key Features

- Native-Feel Archiving: Adds an "Archive" option directly into the Gemini chat context menu (the three-dot menu).
- Decluttered Sidebar: Instantly hide completed or low-priority conversations from your main view.
- Dedicated Archive Section: Access all your hidden chats in a cleanly integrated "Archived" area within the Gemini interface.

## How it works

- Navigate to gemini.google.com.
- Click the three dots next to any chat in your history.
- Select "Archive" to hide it from the main chat history.
- View or restore your chats anytime from the "Archived Chats" section.
